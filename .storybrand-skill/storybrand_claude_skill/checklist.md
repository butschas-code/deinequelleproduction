QA Checklist (run after output)

Content accuracy:
- [ ] Every factual claim exists verbatim (or clearly) in the provided website text
- [ ] No invented numbers, certifications, or locations
- [ ] All services/offers from the source are included (none dropped)

StoryBrand structure:
- [ ] Customer is hero; brand is guide
- [ ] Desire is ONE primary desire
- [ ] Problem includes external/internal/philosophical (source-backed)
- [ ] Plan is exactly 3 steps
- [ ] CTA is clear and repeated
- [ ] Failure is 1 light sentence
- [ ] Success is grounded and specific

Tone + readability:
- [ ] Calm, understated European tone
- [ ] Fifth-grade reading level; short sentences; no filler
- [ ] No hype/urgency/drama

Schema:
- [ ] Reviews included in JSON-LD or marked missing
